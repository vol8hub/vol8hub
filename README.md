# Vol8Hub

**Peer intelligence for professional pilots preparing for recurrent simulator checks.**

vol8hub.com is a community-run resource where active line pilots share recent simulator check experiences to help colleagues prepare. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any airline, aviation authority, or training organisation.

---

## What This Is

- A searchable archive of real pilot sim experiences across multiple fleet types
- Filterable by fleet, check type, cycle, and year
- An anonymous submission channel for pilots who want to contribute without being identified
- An email notification service for new entries — no account required

## What This Is Not

- Not an official airline resource
- Not a union publication
- Not a substitute for your airline's operations manuals, SOPs, or official training publications
- Not a social network or job board

---

## Contributing

Pilots wishing to contribute a recent sim experience can submit anonymously via the Submit page at vol8hub.com/submit.html. All submissions are reviewed before publication. Contributor identity is never disclosed without explicit written consent.
